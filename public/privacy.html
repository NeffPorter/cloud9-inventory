Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Cloud9 Inventory ("we," "us," or "our") — operated by [Your Legal Name / Business Name] — collects, uses, and protects information when you use our inventory management application (the "App"). The App connects to Clover point-of-sale systems to help merchants manage inventory, track sales, and schedule discounts.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not store plaintext passwords.

Clover merchant data. When you connect a Clover store, we store your Clover Merchant ID and API token in order to communicate with the Clover API on your behalf. We also sync and store the following data from your Clover account: item names, item prices, item costs, stock quantities, item group names, and order/sales data delivered via Clover webhooks.

Usage data. We may collect basic server logs (IP address, request timestamps, error logs) for debugging and security purposes. We do not use analytics tracking services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and operate the App. Specifically:

– To sync, display, and update your inventory data on Clover.
– To display sales analytics and suggested reorder quantities within the App.
– To apply and cancel discount schedules you configure, including updating item prices and names on Clover.
– To authenticate your account and secure your session.
– To diagnose errors and improve the stability of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the App.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or your Clover merchant data with third parties, except:

– Service providers. We use Supabase (database hosting) and Railway (application hosting) to operate the App. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies. They do not have permission to use your data for any purpose other than providing services to us.
– Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database. We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Your Clover API token is stored in our database and used only to make authorized API calls on your behalf. We apply industry-standard security practices, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data and Clover merchant data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email address below and we will process your request within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time by contacting us. You may also disconnect your Clover store from the App at any time, after which we will no longer sync data from that merchant account.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by business owners and their employees and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
